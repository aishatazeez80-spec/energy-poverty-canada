Canada's Energy Landscape

Canada as an Energy Leader

Canada is one of the world’s largest energy producers and exporters. According to the Canadian Centre for Energy Information, Canada produces energy from a diverse mix of sources including natural gas, hydroelectricity, wind, solar, oil, and nuclear power.

Energy Poverty in Canada

Energy poverty is not only a global issue , it exists within Canada as well. Research from Efficiency Canada shows that many Canadian households spend a disproportionate percentage of their income on energy costs, especially in colder regions.

Canada's Global Role

Natural gas plays a significant role in Canada’s energy system. It is widely used for electricity generation, heating, and industrial production. Canada also has significant renewable energy capacity, especially in hydroelectric power. This diverse energy portfolio positions Canada as both an energy supplier and an innovator.
